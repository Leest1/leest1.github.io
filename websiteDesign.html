Website Design

based in Irvine, CA

My process

Clients either need a new look and feel or a brand new website from scratch. Depending on their needs, their target audience, and their branding, I work with the client to fulfill their vision, while creating a delightful, visually appealing, and accessible website.

Some things I consider:

  • site audits
  • user flows
  • competitive research
  • target audience
  • accessibility (color contrast, alt text, semantic HTML)

Websites

Refresh TMS

Refresh TMS is a mental health facility who's site needed a visual overhaul. This was my first project at Webserv and was a wonderful learning experience to put design principles into practice.

3 Thrones Hospitality

3 Thrones is a hospitality investment firm that was looking for a portfolio site. The challenge was to create attractive and unique design to showcase their investments and properties.

Villa Oasis San Diego

Villa Oasis is a drug and alcohol treatment center that wanted to redesign their site to highlight their incredible facility and the level of care that they offer.

Hydrate Now

HydrateNow is an IV therapy medical spa that offers a variety of services for beauty and wellness. They came to us for a complete rebrand and improved user experience.

ABC Wellness Group

ABC Wellness Group serves as a psychiatric facility dedicated to offering treatment and care to individuals within their community. Their goal was to rebrand along with creating a new website.

Finish Line Recovery

Finish Line Recovery is an upscale sober living environment with multiple residences in LA County. The client was looking for rebrand along with their new site.

Inner Minds Health

Inner Minds Health is a mental health and eating disorder treatment center. In this project, I created their logo and established their company's branding & design language.

Pierpont Inn

Pierpont Inn is a historic hotel by the Ventura Pier in California. With it's rich history, my client was looking for a look-and-feel refresh and improved user experience, while preserving the established branding.

Summus Laser

Summus Laser is the leading provider of laser therapy machines. This company was going through a massive rebrand and site overhaul. The main challenge of this site was including both B2B and B2C interfaces, and considering the unique user journeys of each user group.
